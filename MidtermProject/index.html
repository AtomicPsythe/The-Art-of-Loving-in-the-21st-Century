Project Abstract

A interactive digital experience exploring that explores the 8 classical forms of love through narrative, color, and user interaction. Invites users to rediscover the quiet, powerful ways love exists in friendship, family, strangers, and within themselves.

Figma Design

The Figma design translates the conceptual framework into a cohesive interactive layout, mapping typography, color systems, and user flows across each love type. It visualizes the emotional journey from exploration to self-reflection through structured pages and intentional design.

Style Guide

A style guide defining the color palettes, typographies, and interactive elements. It ensures that each form of love is expressed through a distinct yet harmonious style, reinforcing the emotional narrative across the website.
